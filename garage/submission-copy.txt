One-liner
Make a one-line promise to a friend — they witness it, you close the loop, optional CRC thanks. Circle + High Fives track who keeps their word.

Circles integration
- @aboutcircles/miniapp-sdk — wallet connect, account creation, CRC transfers
- requestCreateAccount() + referral invite links when sharing promises
- constructAdvancedTransfer() — optional 1 CRC thanks after a promise is kept
- getProfileView() — profiles on promise cards
- Runs in Circles playground + miniapps iframe host

Note for reviewers
No mic, no popups, no “open in browser” flows. Entire loop works inside the Circles iframe.
